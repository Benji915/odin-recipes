Lobster Lasagna

Description

This is truly a unique dish that deviates from its classic Italian origin. This Lobster Lasagna is a truly unique innovation that combines seafood, tradition, and cheese. The preparation is quite simple. It just requires 5 minutes of preparation and 25 minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive olive
  • 1/4 cup(1.2 ounces) leek
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 teaspoons dry white wine
  • 1/2 pound cooked lobster meat
  • 1/4 cup fresh ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup Alfredo sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons tomato paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)

      • Steps to Make It

      1. Heat the olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat
      2. Add the leek and cook until softened, two to three minutes
      3. Add the garlic and white wine and continue cooking until garlic is softened, another two to three minutes
      4. Remove from heat and let cool
      5. In a large bowl, mix all the remaining lobster filling ingredients
      6. Then add in teh cooled leek-garlic mixture and mix to combine
      7. Set aside
