Eggs and Bacon Wrap

What makes this Plate of Eggs and Bacon Different?

A staple cuisine for breakfast, this recipe of eggs and bacon will not only fill the void of hungry but send you to a savory bliss.

Ingredients Needed

  • Flour Tortillas
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Cook the bacon to your liking
  2. Then cook your eggs. Preferably scrambled but anyway will work!
  3. Fold the eggs and bacon in a tortilla without ripping the tortilla.
  4. Enjoy!
